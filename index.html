Seminars & Talks

Abstract

This talk explores the application of Hawkes processes, a type of self-exciting point process, to model financial market volatility. We will discuss how clustering of trading events can be captured by this framework and present empirical results from analyzing high-frequency order book data. The session will cover both the theoretical underpinnings and practical implementation challenges.
