Journal Club Guidelines

For Presenters

  • • Prepare a 20-30 minute presentation
  • • Include key contributions and limitations
  • • Share presentation materials one week in advance
  • • Lead discussion session and Q&A

For Participants

  • • Review paper before presentation
  • • Actively participate in discussions
  • • Come prepared with questions
  • • Share related papers or resources
