Frequently Asked Questions

Ph.D. students receive full tuition coverage and a competitive monthly stipend. M.S. students may receive partial support through research assistantships. Additional funding is available through various scholarships and industry partnerships.

M.S. programs typically take 2 years to complete. Ph.D. programs usually require 4-5 years, including coursework and dissertation research. Combined M.S.-Ph.D. programs are also available.

While finance experience is beneficial, it's not required. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds including mathematics, physics, computer science, and engineering. Strong quantitative skills and programming ability are more important than specific finance knowledge.

Our graduates pursue diverse careers including quantitative researcher/trader positions at hedge funds, data scientist roles at tech companies, risk management positions at banks, and academic positions at universities. Many also join fintech startups or consulting firms.
